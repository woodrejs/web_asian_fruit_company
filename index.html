our product range

Our products are grown and packed according to market trends and the specific of our clients. Our Growers regularly test the fruits and vegetables in the field and ensure they are delivered at the moment when they have an optimal level of ripeness. A balance is therefore always found between the shelf life of the product and the taste, colour and juice content required by our clients.
